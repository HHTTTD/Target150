Clinical Application

Skin & Hair

Reducing wrinkles and fine lines

AFS stimulates collagen and elastin production in the skin, smoothing the skin and reducing wrinkles.

Skin rejuvenation

AFS repairs and rejuvenates skin tissues damaged by sun exposure or aging, making the skin appear youthful and reducing wrinkles.

Increasing skin hydration and elasticity

AFS improves skin structure, making it firmer and more hydrated.

Scar, acne, and acne scar treatment

AFS repairs damaged tissues, reducing scars and making acne scars less noticeabie.

Stimulating new cell production

AFS stimulates new skin cell production, resulting in healthier and brighter skin.

Secretion of growth factors

AFS can secrete growth factors that inhibit melanin production, potentially benetting those with pigmentation concerns like melasma.

Increasing hair density

Using AFS to treat hair loss stimulates new hair growth, making hair thicker and stronger.

Anti-Aging

For healthy individuals, research supports the use of AFS for body rejuvenation in adults, offering several benefits:

  • Improved physical
  • Enhanced internal repair
  • Reduced fatigue
  • Better exercise capability due to improved lung function from cell rejuvenation
  • Overall improved quality of life

**Anti-aging medicine not only focuses on treating aging but also promotes long-term health and quality of life.**

Potential for Treating Various Diseases

Liver Diseases

Repairing liver tissues damaged by cirrhosis or hepatitis.

Kidney Diseases

Regenerating kidney tissues damaged by injury or degeneration.

Heart Diseases

Repairing heart muscle damaged by heart failure or ischemic disease.

Lung Diseases

Regenerating lung tissues damaged by COPD or pneumonia.

Bone and Joint Diseases

Repairing damaged bone and joint tissues.

Neurological Diseases

Treating conditions like stroke, Parkinson's disease, and spinal cord injuries.

Diabetes

Regenerating insulin-producing pancreatic cells.

Immune System Disorders

Improving immune function and reducing inflammation.
