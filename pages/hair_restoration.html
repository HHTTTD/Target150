Hair Restoration

Hair loss is a common medical condition that can affect up to 70% of men and about 40% of women may also face this issue.

Currently, an advanced cellular regeneration process is being developed to treat this condition, using ‘hair follicle stem cell injections.’ The stem cells are injected to stimulate new hair growth, restoring the scalp to its previous healthy condition. Not only does this help regrow thicker hair, but it also protects hair follicles from the hormone Dihydrotestosterone (DHT), one of the male hormones that is a leading cause of hair loss and baldness.
